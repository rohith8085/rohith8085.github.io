About Me

I am a dedicated Software QA Engineer and BSc Computer Science graduate from the University Institute of Technology, Alappuzha. With a sharp analytical mindset and a deep commitment to quality, I have built a career ensuring that software systems perform flawlessly — from the first line of code to the final production release.

My journey in quality assurance has taken me across diverse environments: intensive SDET training at QSpiders Kochi, real-world project delivery at Motionray Marketing Consultancy, and complex enterprise travel-tech systems at Caxita Tech Solutions — where I currently serve as a Junior Software Tester with 7+ months of hands-on experience.

I specialize in designing comprehensive test strategies, crafting both manual and automated test suites, and building robust Selenium-based frameworks using the Page Object Model pattern. I have deep domain knowledge in travel technology — having tested flight booking engines, hotel reservation systems, car rental platforms, sports ticketing, travel packages, insurance modules, activities, and transfer management systems integrated with globally recognized GDS and OTA providers.

Beyond traditional QA, I stay ahead of the curve by actively leveraging AI tools and prompt engineering to supercharge testing productivity — from AI-generated test case suggestions to intelligent defect analysis workflows. I believe the future of QA lies at the intersection of human expertise and intelligent automation.
